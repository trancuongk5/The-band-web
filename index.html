THE BAND

We love music

Blackpink is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of four members: Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. The group is the most successful Korean girl group internationally, dubbed as the "biggest girl group in the world." They have achieved numerous impressive feats, including topping the Billboard 200 chart with the album Born Pink, and their song Pink Venom reaching number one on the ARIA Singles Chart. This has also helped them establish multiple Guinness World Records, as well as receiving numerous international awards and recognition from prestigious music associations such as the RIAA and BPI.
